Welcome To The FAQ Page

How To Navigate Corner-EV



First time on Corner EV? It's easy! Just enter in the location you would like find chargers/fuel in the search bar. As you are typing your location, it should come up and just click on that. Next, you'll want to make sure that you select the search radius from the location. After that just select wheather you would like to find chargers, or an alternative fuel.

Keep in mind there are many different types of EV charge companies to select from. There are also different types of chargers. Please check the Charging And Fuel Information tab at the top for for information on which type might best suit you!

Commonly Asked Questions



If I find and issue with the page, is there a place I can report it?

Yes! Please visit our contact page to reach out regarding an issue


Where can I find information regarding different fuel and charge types?

You can find more information and links HERE or at our Charging And Fuel Information page in the top right corner.


Do I have to make an account to use Corner EV?

No! Corner EV is free to use for everyone.


How up to date are the locations for charging and fuel?

Corner EV uses HERE Developer's API for searches, and is up to date as of today!


What cities can I search?

Corner EV allows you to seach any city, or address in the United States. You can also click on the use my current location button for a search near you.
