Fuel Types

Biodiesel

This is a renewable fuel that can be made from animal fats, vegetable oils, or recycled cooking oil for use in diesel vehicles

Learn More

Electricity

Electricity can be used to power plug-in hybrid as well as electric vehicles. In North America, there are three connector types: J1772, CCS1, and Tesla.

Learn More

Ethanol

Ethanol is a widely used renewable fuel from corn and other plant materials. Different vehicle makes and models require specific ethanol contents.

Learn More

Hydrogen

Hydrogen is a less common emissions free alternative fuel that is produced from domestic resources for us in vehicles with fuel cells.

Learn More

Natural Gas

Natural gas is a domestically abundant gas-based fuel that has significant fuel cost savings over gasoline and diesel fuels.

Learn More

Propane

Propane is a readily available gas-based fuel that is well known and has been used in vehicles for many years.

Learn More
